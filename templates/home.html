Interesting Facts about Cricket

Cricket is the second most popular sport in the world

Cricket is played in over 100 countries and has over 2.5 billion fans worldwide.

Women’s cricket was played as early as the 18th century

The first recorded women's cricket match was played in 1745 between two villages in Surrey, England.

The longest cricket match lasted 14 days

The match was played between England and South Africa in 1939 and ended in a draw after 10 days of play.
